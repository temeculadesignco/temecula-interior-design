03 — The Difference

Not just decorated. Engineered to build.

Most interior designers stop at the mood board. Every cabinet run, appliance, and fixture is measured, scaled, and specified before a single item is ordered — the same coordinated set that clears plan-check also drives procurement and installation. Three arms of one firm, one accountable team.

  • ◆ As-built field measurement & existing-condition documentation
  • ◆ Site & setback plans with dimensioned property clearances
  • ◆ Existing & proposed floor, roof & elevation plans
  • ◆ Dimensioned cabinetry, appliance & fixture schedules
  • ◆ Structural engineer coordination & city plan-check submittals
  • ◆ Remodel & construction management through completion
  • ◆ Furniture, fixtures & décor sourced, delivered & placed
