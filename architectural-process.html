The Process

One residence,
start to finish.

How it Happens

A single Temecula Valley estate project — from a bare vineyard parcel through completion. Designed, drawn, managed, and furnished by one firm.

Most projects lose something in the handoffs — the architect draws it, a builder interprets it, and a third party furnishes it months later. We work the other way. The plan set for this residence was produced by Temecula Interior Design, and the same firm carries it through construction management and fulfillment. What follows is that project, phase by phase, and the reasoning behind each step.

The Sequence

From bare site to completion.

One vantage point, held across the life of the project.

Phase 01

Drafted Framework & Design Theory

Before a trench is dug, the residence already exists as a complete, coordinated set. Siting is resolved against setbacks, grade, and the view corridor; structure is integrated into clean architectural sheets that pass plan-check. Everything downstream answers to this document — which is why it is worth getting right before anyone breaks ground.

Phase 02

Build Begins

Foundation and framing go in exactly as drawn. Because the plan set was produced in-house, our project management team reads the drawings in the field directly — there is no interpretation gap between the designer's intent and the framer's tape measure, and no waiting on an outside office to answer a question.

Phase 03

Structure Up

Roof planes, envelope, and openings take shape. Field questions get resolved against the same set that cleared plan-check, so trades stay coordinated and the massing that was drawn is the massing that actually gets built. Proportion survives contact with the job site.

Phase 04

Construction

Systems, close-in, and interior rough-in run in parallel. This is where fulfillment starts earning its keep: long-lead finishes, fixtures, and furnishings are scheduled against the construction calendar so that nothing sits on the critical path waiting for a delivery.

Phase 05

Near Completion

Hardscape, pool, and landscape complete the site strategy set back in Phase 01. Interior finishes install alongside them, so the exterior program and the interior program land together rather than sequentially — the house and its setting are finished as one project, not two.

Phase 06

Completion

Punch, final styling, and handover. The residence reads as a single continuous idea because the plans, the project management, and the fulfillment all come from the same firm. One accountable team from first sketch to final walkthrough.
