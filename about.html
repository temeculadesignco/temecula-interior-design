Our Story

Building you a better life.

We founded Temecula Interior Design on a simple conviction: great spaces deserve one team that can both imagine them and build them.

From our studio on Winchester Road, we serve homeowners and businesses throughout the Temecula Valley. What sets us apart is range — we move fluidly from mood and material into dimensioned, permit-ready architectural documentation, then coordinate furnishings and construction so nothing gets lost in translation between designer, city, and builder.

Every project is led personally by our principal, with a small, senior team and a network of trusted structural, remodel, and fulfillment partners behind each engagement.
